Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

A boy, Tanjiro Kamado, becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his sister, Nezuko, transformed into a demon. He joins the Demon Slayer Corps, seeking vengeance and a cure. Tanjiro battles demons, uncovers mysteries, and strives to eradicate the dark forces that destroyed his life, determined and unyielding.
